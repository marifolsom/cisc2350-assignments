Marisa Folsom

ABOUT

Marisa is a twenty-one-year-old aspiring photographer and designer born and raised in Tokyo, Japan, currently studying at Fordham University at Lincoln Center in New York City. She is double majoring in Visual Arts and New Media & Digital Design, concentrating in Photography and Art, Text, and Design, respectively. Photography has been a passion of hers since highschool, and although she came into university with the intention of becoming a teacher, she realized art was the direction she wanted to head into. Last summer, she spent 7 weeks studying abroad in Rome, Italy, taking a Documentary Photography class where her and her classmates compiled a 68-page book, Documentary Photography: Italy 2016.

Marisa has a twin sister who is studying nursing at New York University, as well as two younger half siblings (ages five and three) that are currently living in Hong Kong. Her mother is from Hokkaido, the northernmost island of Japan, and her father was born in Ithaca, New York. She has a thirteen-year-old pet dog named Chelsea, who is a whippet, with whom she enjoys snuggling with on the couch while watching TV. Speaking of TV, Marisa loves watching TV and is currently hooked on Mozart in the Jungle, Westworld, and Veep.
